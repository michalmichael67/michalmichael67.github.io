Bellybutton Biodiversity Application Will belly button bacteria identify the next beef alternative?

Improbable Beef, a new plant-based beef company is looking for the best bacteria to create meat-like taste and texture for their product.

In the search, scientists have hypothesized that the best bacteria for this purpose can be found in the navel. In this dataset, volunteers had their navels analyzed and the top 10 bacterial species were identified. This way, if Improbable Beef finds a bacteria candidate, the scientists will be able to identify which volunteers have that strain in their navel.
